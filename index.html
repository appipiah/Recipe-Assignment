Piah's Scrambled Eggs Recipe

I've been told I make really tasty scrambled eggs and today, I'd like to expose my not-so-secret recipe!

The Nitty-Gritty Eggy Details

Preparation time: 5 - 10 minutes

Cooking time: 15 - 20 minutes

Servings: 2

Difficulty level: Beginner (Anybody can make it)

What You’ll Need

  • 4 Eggs
  • 2 roughly blended Peppers (I recommend Bell Peppers)
  • 1/2 cup Chopped Onion
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 Seasoning cube (I recommend Chicken Flavor Tasty Cubes )
  • 1/2 cup of Vegetable oil
  • Pan for frying (duh)
  • Optional: Vegetable, Cheese

Steps to make it

So, you have your ingredients. Now it’s time to cook!

  1. Prepare Ingredients:
    Round up all your ingredients. Break eggs into a medium bowl, do not whisk.

  2. Sauté some Onions:
    Drizzle a bit of oil into your frying pan and warm it over low heat;
    Toss in most of the chopped onions (reserve a few for garnish), crumble in the seasoning cube to taste;
    Fry on low heat for about 3 minutes until fragrant and just turning golden.

  3. Cook the Pepper:
    Pour in the chopped peppers and fry for about 2 minutes. You want them lively and warmed, not mushy.

  4. Add Eggs:
    Pour your unwhisked eggs into the pepper-onion mix. Let the egg set a bit before stirring it gently to scramble;
    Cook until it’s fully set and lightly fry-kissed. This should take about 3 minutes

  5. Season and Finish:
    Add salt to taste. If you’ve got veggies or cheese, sprinkle them in now and stir just enough to warm or melt them in.
    Then, serve.

Things to Note

  • I prefer not to whisk my eggs before cooking; this gives them a chunkier, more textured feel.
  • You can use any type of pepper or onions. I prefer Bell peppers and red onions
  • If using cheese, add it just before the eggs finish cooking so it melts smoothly.
  • For a healthier option, substitute olive oil for vegetable oil.
  • Serve immediately for the best flavor.
